WE ARE

ListedBlue is an exclusive collection of meticulously sourced, hand-made, highly individual objects that have narrowly escaped human obliteration.

The Collection invokes a renewed sensation of Value in a time when our product & production ecosystems are about to disrupt ... indefinitely.

WE OFFER

As representatives of an era before creation became production, ListedBlue objects have never claimed any economical value. By offering 3 embodiments of each of the Collections’ pieces we offer identification & contribution to this archaeological ethos.

Three embodiments include:

  1. THE OBJECT: the unique, hand-made piece
  2. THE IMAGE: a series of 10 print showing three perspectives of the Object in 1:1 size
  3. THE DOWNLOAD: an infinitely - 3D printable - scan of the Object

Ordered through us each of our products ship with a ListedBlue certificate of authenticity. As per request we can provide Image framing & 3D printing services.
