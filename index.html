WE PRESENT

ListedBlue, an exclusive collection of meticulously sourced, hand-made, highly individual objects that have narrowly escaped human obliteration.

The Collection invokes a renewed sensation of value in a time when our product & production ecosystems are about to disrupt ... indefinitely.

WE OFFER

As representatives of an era before creation became production, ListedBlue objects have claimed neither economic nor monetary value. By offering 3 embodiments of each of the Collections’ pieces we offer identification & contribution to this archaeological ethos.

Each piece in the collection is available in 3 distinct product embodiments:

  1. THE OBJECT
    The unique, hand-made piece
  2. THE IMAGE
    Portraying 3 of the objects' perspectives in 1:1 scale (series 10)
  3. THE DOWNLOAD
    Infinitely - 3D printable - scan of the Object

Ordered through us each of our products ship with a ListedBlue certificate of authenticity.
We provide print-framing & 3D print services per request.
